𓇼 ziyu's biography 𓇼

ziyu, also known by his birth name zheng peng, is an actor, singer, and dancer born on july 6, 2002, in lianyungang, jiangsu province, china.
on may 28, 2019, he became a trainee with the entertainment company huakai banxia. to pursue his dream, he dedicated himself fully, going from school to the practice room, where he often trained until the early hours of the morning. on june 26, 2020, he participated as a huakai banxia trainee in "We Are Young", a talent show for boy groups. in january 2021, he participated, also as a huakai banxia trainee, in "Youth With You 3", a reality show for selecting and forming boy groups produced by iqiyi. however, he didn't make it into the final group.

on june 25, 2021, he officially joined the group guanghe shaonian, marking his official debut. however, his time there was short-lived, as he was harassed for an extended period by the group's leader. on december 23, he began talks with huakai banxia to terminate his contract. on january 8, 2023, huakai banxia demanded, without prior notice, that he pack his personal belongings and move to hangzhou by the 14th. on january 9, he released a statement in response to the harassment incident, confirming that he had been assaulted by zheng xingyuan, the leader, and announced that he would file a lawsuit and terminate his contract with the company. although he ultimately succeeded in terminating his contract, he was fined 600,000 chinese yuan, or approximately US$88,062.12, which he must repay within five years.

in june 2025, the TV series "revenged love" (逆爱) began to premiere, which made ziyu gain recognition, both for his talent and for his constant efforts to succeeded.

currently, he is living in beijing, china, along with his boyfriend tian xuning (田栩宁), his co-star from "revenged love".
